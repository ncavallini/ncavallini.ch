## Overview

A data-driven web application that recommends Zurich neighborhoods based on user lifestyle preferences, aimed at helping newcomers navigate the city.

## What it does

Users input their priorities — commute time, proximity to green spaces, nightlife options, cost of living — and the app analyzes data across Zurich's neighborhoods to suggest the best matches. Built to address the common challenge newcomers face when choosing where to live in Zurich.

## Tools Used

TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Node.js
